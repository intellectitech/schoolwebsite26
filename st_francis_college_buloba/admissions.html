Admissions Process

We seek to admit students with academic desire, active curiosity, and interest in community values. Here is our 4-step enrolment timeline:

1

Submit Inquiry

Contact us via phone, email, or visit our campus in Buloba to ask questions and take brochures.

2

Fill Application

Complete the online form below or visit the admissions office to fill out a physical application package.

3

Assessment

Submit previous report cards. Entrance assessment tests may be scheduled for new candidates.

4

Enrolment

Upon review, successful candidates receive an admission package containing fees payment requirements.

Enrolment Requirements

Compulsory Documents

  • Completed & signed admission application form.
  • 3 recent colored passport photographs.
  • Previous term report card or UNEB result slip (UCE/PLE).
  • Recommendation letter from previous headteacher.

Health & Medical Checks

  • Certified medical check-up report from recognized clinic.
  • Completed immunization history logs.
  • Clear declaration of chronic allergies or specific health needs.
