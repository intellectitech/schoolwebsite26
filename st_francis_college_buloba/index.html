Welcome to Our College

Empowering Generations Since 2015

Located in Buloba, Uganda, St. Francis College Buloba has built a formidable reputation as a hub for secondary education. Our mission is to mold young minds into competent, responsible, and god-fearing citizens who can contribute positively to a globalized society.

With an emphasis on academic diligence, moral uprightness, and active co-curricular participation, we cultivate a vibrant community where every learner is guided to realize their full potential.
