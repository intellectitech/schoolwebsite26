Campus Highlights

Modern Classrooms

Well-spaced, clean learning rooms with boards, reading chairs, and storage spaces built for active studies.

Resource Center

Our extensive collection of reference books, subject study guides, newspapers, and computers with network access.

Science Laboratories

Chemistry, Physics, and Biology workrooms with appropriate gas line hooks, beakers, chemical resources, and equipment.

Sports Facilities

A full-size football field, lanes for running track lanes, and a concrete court built for basketball training.

Boarding Houses

Clean, secure residential facilities with individual lockers, bed space, bath facilities, and guidance by resident house masters.

Dining Hall

A spacious assembly hall and dining room offering balanced, fresh foods prepared daily by our catering department.
