Our Story & Leadership

Discover the values, history, and team that define St. Francis College Buloba

Our Rich History

Founded in 2015, St. Francis College Buloba began with a vision to deliver premium, affordable secondary education focused on holistic human development. Starting with just a handful of students and a dedicated band of educators, the college has steadily expanded its campus infrastructure and academic offerings.

Over the past decade, we have established ourselves as one of the premier educational institutions along Mityana Road. We are fully registered and classified by the Ministry of Education and Sports, offering both UNEB curriculum and DIT vocational skills.

Today, our campus serves a diverse community of over 1,000 students. Our graduates have transitioned successfully to major national and international universities, embodying our motto: "A better world through education."

Our Mission

To provide top quality education that prepares students for a quality life, spirit of enterprise, community-based services, and competent leadership.

Our Vision

To be a model secondary school in the provision of quality education and produce God-fearing, innovative, and responsible citizens.

Our Core Values

Excellence

We pursue the highest standards in academics, administration, and personal conduct, challenging every student to excel.

Integrity

We cultivate moral uprightness, transparency, and honesty in all individual and institutional relationships.

Faith

We believe in nurturing spiritual growth, respect for religious diversity, and strong moral principles.

Innovation

We encourage creative thinking, entrepreneurial spirits, and the integration of technical vocational skills.

Community

We build a supportive, inclusive, and loving family environment where respect and team spirit thrive.

Service

We instil a sense of duty to serve the community, preparing students to be active citizens who solve societal problems.
